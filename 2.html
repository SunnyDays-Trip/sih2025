About Mitti Sewa

Mitti Sewa is an AI-powered crop advisory system designed for small and marginal farmers in Punjab. Our system provides:

  • Personalized crop advice
  • Weather forecasts and alerts
  • Pest and disease identification
  • Soil health management
  • Market price information
  • Fertilizer scheduling
  • Irrigation guidance
  • Community knowledge sharing
  • Crop rotation recommendations
  • Government scheme information

Our mission is to empower farmers with technology-driven solutions to improve yield, reduce costs, and increase profitability.
